Shanghai

Location: Shanghai, China

Status: Living here since 2022

Coordinates: 31.2304° N, 121.4737° E

About

Shanghai is where I work and live. This vibrant metropolis blends traditional Chinese culture with ultra-modern architecture. From the historic Bund to the futuristic skyline of Lujiazui, Shanghai never fails to amaze.

Highlights

  • The Bund - Iconic waterfront with colonial architecture
  • Lujiazui - Home to the Oriental Pearl Tower and Shanghai Tower
  • French Concession - Tree-lined streets with cafes and boutiques
  • Yu Garden - Classical Chinese garden in the Old City

Photos

Notes

Living in Shanghai as a tech professional has been an incredible experience. The city’s energy is unmatched, and the food scene is amazing. My favorite spots include:

  • Morning coffee at a French Concession cafe
  • Weekend walks along the Suzhou Creek
  • Late night coding sessions with a view of the Pudong skyline
